Porridge Yam

(Asaro)

    Serving:
  • 1/2 tuber yam (medium)
  • 1 cup shredded smoke chicken
  • 1/2 cup dry oporo
  • 2 knorr Cube
  • 1 thsp grounded CrayFish
  • 8 hobonero pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cskp palm oil
  • 2 cups diced scent leaf
    Prep time >7mins
  • Peel yam, cube it and set aside
  • Wash and shed pepper roughly and set aside
  • Wash oporo and soak and then set aside
